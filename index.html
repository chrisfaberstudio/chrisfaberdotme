I'm a computer graphics artist and visual researcher with 30 years in the CGI, VFX, and post-production industry - working with world-class brands, founding/growing a studio, and learning that the most interesting images aren't the ones that answer questions. They're the ones that make you feel something before you understand why.

Today I run chrisfaber.studio to help brands, product designers and design teams create visuals that tell the story behind their products, rather than just showing the object. I work from CAD data, before a product exists, turning my clients' vision into form.

Based between Munich and Bali - one place gives me structure, the other gives me space. Both show up in the work.

My process is structural intuition - disciplined experimentation inside defined constraints. I use AI not as a shortcut, but as a collaborator. The human eye, the human decision, the human taste: that's where the image lives.

Let form reveal intention.
